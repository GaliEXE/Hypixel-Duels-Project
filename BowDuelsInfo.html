Bow Duels

Bow Duels is the most simple and easy duel that someone wishing to play duels can do, you have a bow and an Ender Pearl (see ender pearl) and some basic armor. In this duel your main objective is to shoot the other player to death. Typically it only takes 3-4 shoots to kill the opponent. While the concept of Bow Duels is rather simple the hard part is mastering the art of aiming and learning how to not get hit.
