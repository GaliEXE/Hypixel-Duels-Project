What is Hypixel

It is difficult to explain what Hypixel Duels is if you do no know what Hypixel is. Hypixel is a server on a popular game called Minecraft. On this server players compete in many mini games many of them being PVP games (Player vs Player). In certain games you can level up and earn rewards for playing. It is a really fun server and I would 100% garuntee playing on the server. Unfortunately to play on the server you must own a Minecraft Java edition account which currently costs $26.95. The IP for Hypixel is mc.hypixel.net.

What are Duels

In Hypixel there are many types of mini games that you can play. Duels is one of these mini game modes is duels. These are games where you fight against an opponent under a variety of circumstances. There are a variety of duels to pick from and you can play by yourself or in teams of twos or threes in certain gamemodes. Each game mode alters the way you win the duel the tools and armor you receive and any other special effects that are given that are specific to that duel.
