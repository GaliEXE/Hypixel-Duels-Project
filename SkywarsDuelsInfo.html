Skywars Duels

Skywars Duels are a small imitation of the game Skywars in Hypixel. This duel starts you off on an island with chests that you can loot for food, weapons, blocks, potions, and more. In the Center there is a larger island with more powerful loot chests. You also start off with items depending on what type of kits you have. In Skywars duels all the kits are already unlocked. In the original Skywars you must buy kits with currency you win in games.
