Blitz Duels

Blitz Duels is very similar to Mega Walls Duels (seeMega Walls Duels). Like Mega Walls Duels there are many kits that you can choose from. However the kits are structured different and give you different items that you can play with. The kits are also not balanced the same way that Mega Walls kits are. Mega Walls kits focus on things such as classes (attack, defense, etc). Blitz duels kits are more similar to Skywars duels kits (seeSkywars Duels), in the fact that these kits focus around a theme like archer (where your primary weapon is a Bow and Arrow.
