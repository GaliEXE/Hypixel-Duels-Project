Duels Divisions

In every type of duel you can receive a Duel Division after wining a certain amount of games. When a Duels Division is equiped other users will be able to see this title in
Duels lobbies and games. There is also a Overall Duels division which takes twice as many wins to get then individual titles do.
Here are the Duels Divisions that you can receive and how many wins it takes to receive them.

Division Gamemode Wins Overall Wins
Rookie 50 100
Rookie II 60 120
Rookie III 70 140
Rookie IV 80 160
Rookie V 90 180
